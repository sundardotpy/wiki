Referral rewards or commissions earned from a OBPP company for referring a customer (including family members like your mother) are covered under Section 194H. ([1](https://www.jiraaf.com/blogs/taxation/section-194h-tds-guide), [2](https://www.sahi.com/glossary/section-194h-of-income-tax-act))

The Income Tax Department evaluates the substance of a payout over its commercial name. Even if a company calls it a "reward," "bonus," or "incentive," it functionally acts as a commission for business facilitation or lead generation. ([1](https://busy.in/tds/section-194h-of-income-tax/), [2](https://www.sahi.com/glossary/section-194h-of-income-tax-act))

**Why it is Applicable:**

- **Definition of Commission:** Under [**<u>Section 194H guidelines</u>**](https://www.incometaxindia.gov.in/w/section-194h-34), commission includes any payment received for services rendered in the course of buying or selling goods or facilitating assets/valuable transactions. ([1](https://blog.tdsman.com/2026/06/tds-on-brokerage-and-commission-section-3931-section-194h/), [2](https://carajput.com/blog/commission-booking-in-family-member-low-income-pan-holder/))
- **The Bond Brokerage Exception:** While Section 194H explicitly excludes standard brokerage on stock market securities transactions, it **does apply to referral or marketing incentives** paid out by platforms to individuals acting as intermediaries to source new clients. ([1](https://carajput.com/blog/commission-booking-in-family-member-low-income-pan-holder/), [2](https://www.jiraaf.com/blogs/taxation/section-194h-tds-guide))

**How TDS Rules Apply to Your Reward:**

- **The Threshold Limit:** The bond company will only deduct TDS if your total referral rewards or commissions cross **₹20,000** within a single financial year. If your payout is below this amount, no tax is withheld. ([1](https://www.indiafilings.com/learn/section-194h-tds-on-commission-and-brokerage))
- **The Deduction Rate:** If your earnings cross the ₹20,000 threshold, the company will deduct a **2% TDS** before crediting the money to your account, provided you linked your PAN. ([1](https://taxgarden.in/blog/tds-on-commission-brokerage-section-194h-393-guide-india), [2](https://www.mstock.com/refer-and-earn-terms-and-conditions), [3](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DLkY3_zNw3l/))

**How to Check and Claim This Tax:**

- **Form 26AS / AIS:** The bond platform is required to file a quarterly tax return. Once processed, this deducted tax will automatically reflect under your PAN in your Form 26AS and Annual Information Statement (AIS).
