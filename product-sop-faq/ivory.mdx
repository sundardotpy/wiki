## What is Wint Ivory?

Wint Ivory is a premium program designed for investors who maintain **₹25 lakhs or more in active investments (AUM)**. It offers a more enhanced and personalised investment experience.

## Benefits

Ivory investors receive:

* An additional **0.25% YTM** return on investments
* Access to a **dedicated Relationship Manager**
* More features to be added in future

<Note>
  The extra 0.25% is **not** credited separately — it is adjusted in the investment amount and reflected in the overall YTM. It appears in the dashboard as a strikethrough against the base rate.
</Note>

## Eligibility

You need to maintain an active AUM of **₹25 lakhs or more** on the platform. Once met, Ivory status is assigned **automatically** — no separate action is needed, and it updates shortly after AUM crosses the threshold and bonds are settled.

<Warning>
  Eligibility is based on **active AUM**, not total amount ever invested. Active AUM excludes any repayments and amortised principal.
</Warning>

### AUM examples

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Qualifies — ₹30L fully invested">
    If you have invested ₹30 lakhs and the entire amount is still actively invested (no principal repayments or maturities yet), your active AUM remains ₹30 lakhs. As this is above ₹25 lakhs, you qualify as an Ivory investor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does not qualify — ₹30L invested but partly repaid">
    If you have invested ₹30 lakhs but ₹10–15 lakhs has already been repaid or amortised over time, your current active AUM reduces to ₹15–20 lakhs. As this is below ₹25 lakhs, you do not qualify.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### What counts toward AUM

* Only **bond investments** count toward Ivory eligibility.
* **FDs are not included** in the calculation — even ₹25L combining bonds and FDs does not qualify.
* AUM is the total value of active investments, excluding repayments or returned principal. Users can check it as the **current value** in their [portfolio](https://www.wintwealth.com/portfolio/bonds/).

## Losing and regaining Ivory status

If active AUM drops below ₹25 lakhs, Ivory benefits are **paused**. As soon as active investments go back above ₹25 lakhs, the benefit resumes automatically.

<Warning>
  The 0.25% benefit applies **only to investments made after** the user becomes eligible as an Ivory member. Investments made before eligibility do not retroactively receive the benefit.
</Warning>

## Handling common queries

<Note>
  Ivory eligibility is calculated on an **individual account basis**. It cannot be combined across family members, shared from a parent or sibling's account, granted for long tenure of association, or extended as an exception for AUM just below the threshold (for example ₹24.6 lakhs). Acknowledge the user's expectation, explain the individual-account rule, and note that more programs may be introduced as the platform grows.
</Note>

### Family and shared eligibility

Ivory eligibility and the welcome gift are tied to each investor's **own** active AUM. Even within a family, accounts are treated separately — status and gifts cannot be shared across family members. Combined family investment does not qualify an individual account.

### Duration of association

Ivory eligibility is based on maintaining ₹25L+ active AUM, **not** on how long the user has been an investor. Long-standing investors who do not meet the AUM criteria are not eligible.

### The welcome gift

The welcome gift is part of the Ivory program and is linked to individual Ivory eligibility. It is limited to eligible members and is not offered to all investors, for program-design and cost reasons.

### "Why do only rich investors get benefits?"

Acknowledge how it may feel. The program is structured around investment size so Wint can sustainably provide enhanced returns and dedicated support. Extending it to all investors is not currently feasible. Every investor is valued, and more programs may come as the platform grows.

### Referrals vs Ivory

The referral program and Ivory are **separate** offerings. Referral benefits continue independently; Ivory is based purely on maintaining ₹25L+ active AUM.

## Returns and offers

* **Cannot be combined with other offers** — if a user invests on an offer day, only one benefit applies at a time. The Ivory 0.25% YTM cannot stack with other offers.
* **No strict cap** — the 0.25% YTM benefit applies to eligible Ivory investments across bonds, but cannot be combined with other offers.
* **Combined display** — returns are shown as a combined YTM. A separate base-vs-Ivory breakdown is not displayed yet.
* **Liquidity support** — beyond the 0.25%, Ivory members get dedicated support and quicker assistance for liquidity requests.

Users can verify the benefit via the YTM shown in their portfolio, which already includes the Ivory benefit where applicable, or by contacting their RM.
