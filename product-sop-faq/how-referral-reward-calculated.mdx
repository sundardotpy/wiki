---
title: "How is the Referral Reward Calculated?"
---

The referral reward is a flat 1% of the referee's investment amount.

To calculate the referral reward:

1. Take the referee's total investment amount
2. Calculate 1% of that amount
3. That is the referral reward payable to the referrer

Example:<br />Referee invests: ₹1,00,000<br />Referral Reward = 1% of ₹1,00,000 = ₹1,000

The more your referee invests, the higher your reward — up to a maximum of ₹5,000 per referral.
