### What is the Referral 2X Program?

Referral 2X is a limited-time specific referral campaign where eligible users can earn double referral rewards for successful referrals who sign up within a specified time period.

### How does the referral program work?

1. Referrer shares their referral link/code
2. Referee signs up using the referral link
3. Referee makes an investment
4. Referrer earns referral rewards based on the referee's investment amount

### Who can participate in the Referral 2X Program?

Only users who do not have any existing referral signups are eligible for the Referral 2X campaign.

### Is the Referral 2X Program available to all users?

No. The program is available only to eligible users selected for the campaign. Only users who do not have any existing referral signups are eligible for the Referral 2X campaign.

### How long is the 2X reward offer valid?

The offer remains active until the timer displayed on the referral page expires.

### What is the standard referral reward?

The standard referral reward is 1% of the referee's investment amount. Maximum reward of ₹5,000 per referee.

### What is the 2X referral reward?

During the active timer period, Referrer earns 2% of the referee's investment amount. Maximum reward of ₹10,000 per referee.

### How is the reward calculated?

Reward is calculated as a percentage of the referee's order amount.

Example:<br />Investment Amount = ₹2,00,000

Normal Reward:<br />1% of ₹2,00,000 = ₹2,000

2X Reward:<br />2% of ₹2,00,000 = ₹4,000

### Is there any maximum reward limit per referee?

Yes. The referral reward is subject to a maximum cap per referee:

- Standard Referral: Earn 1% of the referee's investment amount, up to a maximum of ₹5,000
- Referral 2X: Earn 2% of the referee's investment amount, up to a maximum of ₹10,000

If the calculated reward exceeds the applicable cap, the reward will be limited to the maximum amount allowed under the program.

### What happens if the calculated reward exceeds the cap?

The reward will be limited to the applicable maximum cap.

Example:<br />Referee invests ₹8,00,000<br />2% reward = ₹16,000<br />Since the cap is ₹10,000, the referrer receives ₹10,000 only.

### When does the timer start?

The timer starts as soon as the referee visits the referral page.

### Can the timer be paused?

No. Once started, the timer runs continuously until it expires.

### What happens when the timer ends?

Any referee who signs up after the timer expires becomes eligible only for standard referral rewards and not 2X referral reward.

### If a referee signs up before the timer ends but invests later, will I still receive 2X rewards?

Yes. Eligibility for 2X rewards depends on the referee signing up before the timer expires.

### How many referees can earn rewards for a referrer?

Rewards are calculated only for the first 5 referees who invest.

### What if more than 5 referees sign up?

The reward eligibility is determined by the first 5 referees who complete investments.

### What is the maximum referral reward I can earn?

The maximum referral reward you can earn depends on how many of your referrals qualify for the Standard Referral Program and the Referral 2X Program.

- If all 5 eligible referees qualify under the Standard Referral Program, you can earn up to ₹25,000 (₹5,000 per referee).
- If all 5 eligible referees qualify under the Referral 2X Program, you can earn up to ₹50,000 (₹10,000 per referee).

In cases where some referees qualify for 2X rewards and others qualify for standard rewards, your total earnings will be calculated based on the applicable reward cap for each referee.

For example: If 2 referees qualify for 2X rewards and 3 referees qualify for standard rewards, the maximum reward you can earn would be ₹35,000 (₹20,000 from the 2X referrals and ₹15,000 from the standard referrals).

Please note that rewards are calculated only for the first 5 referees who successfully complete an investment.

### What happens if 7 people sign up using my referral link?

Only the first 5 referees who complete investments will be considered for reward calculations.

### 2 referees signed up during the timer and 5 signed up after the timer. Which rewards will I get?

Reward eligibility depends on which referees invest first.

### Two referees are eligible for 2X rewards but do not invest. Five normal referees invest first. What happens?

The first 5 investing referees will be considered.

In this case: All 5 investing referees qualify for normal rewards. Maximum reward potential becomes ₹25,000.

### When will referral rewards be credited?

Rewards will be credited within 7 days after the referee's invested bond is settled.

### Can referral rewards be reversed?

If a transaction is cancelled, failed, or otherwise deemed invalid under referral program terms, the associated reward may be adjusted or reversed.

### Where can I track my referral rewards?

Users can view referral status and rewards within the Referral section of the platform.

### Can Wint modify or discontinue the referral program?

Yes. Wint reserves the right to modify, pause, or discontinue the referral program and its rewards at any time.
