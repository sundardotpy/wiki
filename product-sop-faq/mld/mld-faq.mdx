\n**Q: What is the Marwadi Market Linked Debenture (MLD)?**

A:Marwadi MLD is a listed, secured, rated, and principal-protected investment product whose returns are linked to the performance of the Nifty Midcap 150 Index. It is designed to provide market-linked returns while offering structured downside protection.

**Q:What is the difference between Series A and Series B?**

A: Series A offers 110% participation in the upside of the Nifty Midcap 150 Index with principal protection at maturity. Series B offers a maximum annualized return of 13% (distributed structure), which is reduced only if the Nifty Midcap 150 Index declines during the investment period.

**Q: Which index is the MLD linked to?**

A: Both Series A and Series B are linked to the Nifty Midcap 150 Index, providing diversified exposure to 150 mid-sized companies across various sectors of the Indian economy.

**Q: Why is the product linked to the Nifty Midcap 150 Index instead of the Nifty 50?**

A: The Nifty Midcap 150 Index has historically delivered superior long-term returns compared to the Nifty 50 and offers broader diversification across 150 companies, making it a strong benchmark for long-term wealth creation.

Q: Why is this product suitable for long-term investors?

A: The 3-year investment horizon allows investors to participate in the long-term growth potential of the Nifty Midcap 150 Index while reducing the impact of short-term market volatility through a structured payoff mechanism.

**Returns and Payoff Q: How are returns calculated in Series A?A: If the Nifty Midcap 150 Index appreciates, investors receive 110% participation in the index gains. If the index remains flat or declines, investors receive their principal amount at maturity.**

**Q: How are returns calculated in Series B?**

A: Series B offers a maximum annualized return of 13%. If the index remains flat or rises, investors receive the maximum payout. If the index declines, the maturity value is reduced in proportion to the decline as per the payoff structure.

**Q: What happens if the market performs well?**

A: In Series A, investors benefit from 110% participation in the upside of the Nifty Midcap 150 Index. In Series B, investors receive the maximum applicable maturity value of 13% annualized return.

Q: Is the principal protected?

A: Yes. In Series A, the principal is protected at maturity irrespective of market performance. In Series B, the maturity value is linked to the product payoff structure and reduces only if the underlying index declines.

**Principal Protection and RiskQ: What happens if the market falls?**

A: In Series A, the invested principal remains protected at maturity. In Series B, the maturity value is reduced based on the percentage decline in the underlying index over the investment tenure.

**Q: Can an investor lose money?**

A: Series A protects the invested principal at maturity. In Series B, investors continue to earn positive returns unless the index experiences a significant decline beyond the product's payoff threshold.

Q: What are the key risks associated with this investment?

A: The investment is subject to issuer credit risk, market risk linked to the underlying index, liquidity risk in the secondary market, and taxation as per prevailing laws. Investors should evaluate these risks before investing.

**Issuer Information Q: Who is the issuer of the MLD?**

A: The issuer is Marwadi Chandarana Intermediaries Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (MCIBPL), a part of the Marwadi Chandarana Group with over three decades of experience in financial services and capital markets.

**Q:Why should an investor choose Marwadi as the issuer?**

A: The Marwadi Chandarana Group has a strong financial profile, over three decades of market experience, a combined net worth of approximately ₹2,951 crore, consistent profitability, multiple successful debt issuances, and CRISIL ratings of A+/A with Stable Outlook.

Distribution

Q: Why is the maximum return for Series B 13% and not higher?

A: Series B is offered on Wint Wealth with a maximum annualised return of 13%. The underlying product is structured at 14%, and the difference of 1% accounts for distribution and servicing costs involved in making this product available to investors through this platform. As a result, the maximum cumulative return over the 3-year tenure is approximately 44% through Wint Wealth, compared to approximately 48% on the sourced structure.

**Taxation Q: How are the returns taxed?**

A: As per Section 50AA of the Income Tax Act, gains from these MLDs are treated as Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG) and are taxed at the investor's applicable income tax slab rate, irrespective of the holding period or listing status.

**Listing, Rating and StructureQ: Is the product listed and rated?A: Yes. Both Series A and Series B are listed on BSE, are senior secured instruments, and carry a CRISIL rating of 'A' with a Stable Outlook.**
