---
title: "How to extend the flexi tenure and hold your bond till maturity"
---

To extend the tenure and hold your bond till maturity, please follow these steps below:

1. Click on the Portfolio tab.
2. Click on the Investments Section.
3. Select the bond which you wish to extend the tenure for and hold till maturity.
4. There you will find an option to Extend Tenure.
5. Once you click and confirm the tenure extension, the scheduled sell order for your bond will be automatically cancelled.
