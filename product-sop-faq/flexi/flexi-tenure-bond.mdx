---
title: "Flexi-tenure bond"
---

A Flexible Tenure Bond lets you invest in a longer-term bond (e.g., 18 months) but choose to exit earlier at predefined Tenure options like 3, 6, or 12 months. At the end of your selected Tenure, we’ll try to sell the bond on your behalf and return your funds.

**Flexi Tenure Bond at Scheduled Sell Date Without Buyers**

In the case of a Flexi Tenure bond, if no buyers are available on the pre-scheduled sell date, the bond will automatically continue until its final maturity date. The investor's money remains invested, and there is no loss due to unavailability of buyers.

**Selling Flexi Tenure Bond Before Maturity**

During the tenure of a Flexi Tenure bond, the investor can place a sell order at any time on the platform. If buyers are available, the bond will be sold accordingly.
