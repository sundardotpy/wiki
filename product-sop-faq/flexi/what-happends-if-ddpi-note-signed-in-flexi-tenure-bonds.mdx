---
title: "What happens if DDPI not signed for flexi tenure bonds"
---

DDPI is required for flexi tenure bonds to sell the bond on the choose tenure of the bond automatically. If a user failed to sign DDPI, the bond cannot be sold on the chosen tenure date it extends to maturity. User can sell the bond manually after DDPI is signed or continue holding the bond till maturity.

**Flexi Tenure Bond Without DDPI at Scheduled Sell Date**

<u>If</u> DDPI is not activated at the scheduled sell date, the sell request will not be executed. In this case, the Flexi Tenure bond will automatically continue until its final maturity date. The investor's money remains invested, and there is no loss due to unavailability of buyers or non-execution of the sell request. At maturity, the bond will be redeemed by the issuing entity, and the principal and applicable interest will be paid as per the bond terms.

You may cancel your scheduled SELL order from the app and choose to continue holding the bond until its Actual Maturity. By extending your investment to full maturity, you may earn a higher YTM as per the bond’s original terms.
