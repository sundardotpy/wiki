---
title: "SIP Modification"
description: "Modifying a SIP's amount or deduction date, including mandate limits."
---

SIP amount and date modifications are raised internally. Post the request in `#cx-internal-coordination` or `#cx-live` and tag the TLs.

## Amount modification

Raise the request with:

* **User ID**
* **SIP ID**
* **Amount**

<Warning>
  Check the mandate type (UPI or eNACH) **before** raising an amount modification request.

  * **UPI autopay** mandate supports only **₹10,000 per month** — it cannot be modified. To increase the SIP amount above ₹10,000, the mandate must be **eNACH**.
  * **eNACH** amount modification supports **₹10,000 up to ₹2,90,000**, in increments of ₹10,000.
</Warning>

| Mandate type | Amount range | Modifiable? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| UPI autopay | ₹10,000/month only | No |
| eNACH | ₹10,000 – ₹2,90,000 (increments of ₹10,000) | Yes |

## Date modification

Raise the request with:

* **User ID**
* **SIP ID**
* **Day of month**

<Note>
  SIP date modification is allowed only between the **1st and 28th** of the month.
</Note>
