The **Skip Instalment** feature lets a user skip upcoming instalments without cancelling the entire SIP. The SIP continues, and the user is not charged for the skipped instalment.

<Frame>
  ![SIP Skip Installment](/images/SIP---Skip-Installment.jpg)
</Frame>

## Can I skip my upcoming SIP order?

Users can manage their SIP directly from the Wint app. The **Skip Instalment** feature allows skipping upcoming instalments without cancelling the whole SIP.

<Note>
  A user cannot cancel the **entire** SIP from the app — see [SIP Cancellation](/product-sop-faq/sip/sip-cancellation). They can only skip an instalment, which becomes available once the SIP order is initiated, 4–5 days before the deduction date.
</Note>

## How to skip an instalment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the SIP section">
    Open the **SIP** section in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Skip Instalment">
    Tap the **Skip Instalment** button for the relevant SIP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm on the bottom sheet. The instalment will be skipped.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Users can choose whether or not to skip an instalment each month.

## Skip window

Skipping is only allowed from **T-5 up to T-2** (where T is the deduction date). It closes at T-1 because the autopay charge is raised one day before the instalment.

| Day (example: SIP on 21st March) | Relative day | Skip allowed? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 16th March | T-5 | ✅ Allowed |
| 17th March | T-4 | ✅ Allowed |
| 18th March | T-3 | ✅ Allowed |
| 19th March | T-2 | ✅ Allowed |
| 20th March | T-1 | ❌ Not allowed |
| 21st March | T-0 | ❌ Not allowed |
| 22nd March | T\+1 | No option shown |

<Info>
  **Example** For a SIP on 21st March, the user can skip from 16th March through 19th March. On 20th March (T-1) the option is no longer available. The next skip option becomes visible on **16th April**.
</Info>
