## Face value

The **face value** is the principal amount on which interest is calculated and which is repaid at maturity.

## Discount and premium

A bond bought **below** face value is purchased at a **discount**. A bond bought **above** face value is purchased at a **premium**.

## Clean price vs dirty price

| Term | What it includes |
| --- | --- |
| **Clean price** | Principal plus premium or discount |
| **Dirty price** | Clean price plus [accrued interest](/product-sop-faq/bonds/accrued-interest) |

The dirty price is what the buyer actually pays, since it compensates the seller for interest that has built up since the last coupon payment.

## Acquisition cost

**Acquisition cost** represents the total amount paid over the face value. It is relevant for taxation — see [Taxation](/product-sop-faq/bonds/taxation).

## Sell pricing

When a bond is sold before maturity through the secondary market, the sell price is adjusted so the final return matches the applicable selling YTM at the time of sale. The deduction is not applied as a flat amount — the bond price is adjusted to achieve the applicable selling YTM.

See [Sell charges](/product-sop-faq/bonds/sell-charges) for the full calculation and worked example.
