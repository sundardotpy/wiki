## Are there charges when selling before maturity?

**Q: Are there any charges or deductions when I sell a bond before maturity?**

> There are no brokerage fees for selling bonds on Wint Wealth. However, if you sell a bond before maturity through the secondary market, it may be sold at a YTM up to **1% lower** than your original purchase YTM.
>
> This adjustment helps ensure buyer availability and covers secondary market transaction costs. It only affects the **interest component** — your principal is not impacted. You can always view the exact sell price and applicable YTM before confirming your sell order.

## Which bonds are eligible for Sell Anytime?

The sell feature is available only for bonds purchased through Wint Wealth and held in the NSDL demat account created via Wint Wealth.

<Warning>
  Government Treasury Bills (T-Bills) are **not** eligible for sale through this feature and must be held until maturity — their short remaining tenure makes them less attractive to secondary market buyers.
</Warning>

## SIP investments

**Q: If I withdraw, redeem, or exit my SIP investment, will I be charged a penalty or deduction?**

> No sell deduction applies to SIP investments, whether the investment is redeemed at maturity or withdrawn before maturity.

## Flexi Tenure investments

**Q: Will I be charged a penalty, fee, or YTM deduction if I exit my Flexi Tenure investment early or at the selected tenure?**

> No sell deduction applies when Flexi Tenure investments are redeemed as per the tenure selected at the time of investment, or upon bond maturity.
>
> However, if the underlying bond investment is sold **manually** before the selected tenure or before maturity through the secondary market, a YTM adjustment of up to 1% may apply, similar to other bond sell transactions.

See [Flexi](/product-sop-faq/flexi) for more on Flexi Tenure bonds.

## Sell order execution

**Q: How likely is it that my sell order will actually go through, and how long does it take?**

> Historically, around **98%** of sell orders placed on Wint Wealth are executed within 1 working day, subject to buyer availability.

## How the sell amount is calculated

**Q: How much will I get if I sell my bond early?**

The amount received depends on the difference between the effective buy YTM and the applicable selling YTM at the time of sale:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Baseline">
    Your effective buy YTM is used as the baseline.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Selling YTM">
    At the time of selling, the applicable selling YTM is determined.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Price adjustment">
    The sell price is adjusted so your final return matches that selling YTM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Example** — If you bought a bond at an effective YTM of **11.75%** and choose to sell before maturity, the bond may be sold at an adjusted YTM of **10.75%** — an impact of up to 1% on your effective YTM.

  As a result, the final sell amount may be lower than what you would have received if the bond were held until maturity.
</Info>

<Note>
  The deduction is **not** applied as a flat amount. Instead, the bond price is adjusted to achieve the applicable selling YTM.
</Note>

Users can always view the expected sell amount, sell price, and applicable YTM on the platform before confirming a sell order.
