Use this page to pick the right report for what the user actually wants.

## Self-serve reports

Most report requests can be handled by pointing the user at the download flow.

**Response to user:**

> You can easily download your investment report by following these steps:
>
> 1. Click on **Profile**
> 2. Click on **Account Details**
> 3. Click on **Reports and Documents**
> 4. Click on **Download Investment Summary Report** (or the report you need)

## Requirement to report mapping

| What the user asks for | Report that satisfies it | Where it comes from |
| --- | --- | --- |
| "All my investments in one document" | [Master Report](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/master-report) | Wint Wealth platform |
| "What do I currently hold?" | [Current holdings](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/current-holdings) | Wint Wealth platform |
| "Proof of my holdings for a third party" | [Holding Statement](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/holding-statement) | Ops or NSDL Speed-e |
| "What did I buy, and when?" | [Investment Transactions](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/investment-transactions) | Wint Wealth platform |
| "What did I sell?" | [Sell Transactions](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/sell-transactions) | Wint Wealth platform |
| "What have I been paid so far?" | [Repayments received](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/repayments-received) | Wint Wealth platform |
| "What will I receive, and when?" | [Future cashflows](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/future-cashflows) | Wint Wealth platform |
| "Something for filing my taxes" | [Taxation Report](/product-sop-faq/reports-wint-wealth/taxation-report) | Wint Wealth platform |
| "My CMR / Client Master Report" | CMR — see [Reports - NSDL](/product-sop-faq/reports-nsdl) | Ops or NSDL Speed-e |
| "TDS deducted on my interest" | Form 168 (previously Form 26AS) — see [TDS](/process-sop-faq/tds) | Income Tax portal |

## Reports that require Ops

**CMR** and **Holding Statement** are not generated by the Wint Wealth platform.

**Response to user:**

> Please allow me some time, I will check with the concerned team and get back.

**Agent action:** raise it in `#cx-ops`, ask for the document, share it to the user's **registered** email ID, then inform the user.

<Note>
  Users can also download both the CMR and the holding statement themselves from the **NSDL Speed-e app**.
</Note>
