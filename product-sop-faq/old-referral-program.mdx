---
title: "Old Referral Program"
---

If a Referee created a Wint Wealth account and made an investment within one year, you were eligible for the following rewards:

- 10% of their gains from their first investment
- 5% of their gains from subsequent investments

On August 14th, the National Stock Exchange (NSE) issued a circular prohibiting brokers from offering referral incentives to anyone who is not registered as an authorized person with the exchange. As a result, Wint Wealth has stopped the referral reward payments from September 2024 until we receive further clarification.

We have already paid out all referral rewards due up until August 31st, 2024.
