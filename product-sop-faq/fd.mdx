## Liquidity of Fixed Deposits

Fixed Deposits offer high liquidity and can generally be withdrawn before maturity if required.

### When can an FD be withdrawn?

An FD can be withdrawn after a minimum lock-in period of **7 days** from the date of booking:

* The FD cannot be withdrawn during the first 7 days after booking.
* Once the 7-day lock-in is complete, a withdrawal can be requested at any time before maturity.

## Premature withdrawal

Withdrawing an FD before its maturity date is a **premature withdrawal**. Most FDs allow it, but doing so may reduce the returns earned.

### How interest is recalculated

<Steps>
  <Step title="Recalculation">
    The bank recalculates the interest based on the actual period for which the FD was held.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Penalty">
    A premature withdrawal penalty may be applied — typically an interest rate reduction of up to approximately 1%.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Final credit">
    The final interest credited may be lower than the rate originally booked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Example** — Investment ₹1,00,000 | Tenure 3 years | FD rate 9% p.a.

  If withdrawn after 1 year:

  * The bank will **not** apply the 3-year rate of 9%.
  * It applies the 1-year FD rate applicable at the time of booking — assume 6%.
  * A premature withdrawal penalty of up to 1% may apply.

  **Result:** applicable rate 6% − penalty 1% = **effective interest ~5%** for the period held, instead of the originally booked 9%.
</Info>

The exact penalty depends on the bank's policy and is shown before the withdrawal is confirmed.

## What happens at maturity?

**Q: What happens when my FD matures? Do I need to do anything?**

> No, you do not need to do anything. At maturity, the FD amount along with the applicable interest is automatically credited to your registered bank account by the issuing bank.

## Premature RD liquidation

If a Recurring Deposit is closed before completing the full tenure, the bank does **not** simply deduct a flat penalty from the maturity amount. The entire RD is recalculated, which can significantly impact the final amount received.

### How the RD is recalculated

<Info>
  **Example** — Original tenure 24 months | Original rate 7% p.a. | Closed after 16 months

  1. The bank identifies the applicable interest rate for the actual period the RD was maintained — assume the 16-month rate is 6% p.a.
  2. A premature closure penalty may be applied — for example 1%.

  **Result:** applicable rate 6% − penalty 1% = **effective rate 5%**.
</Info>

### Effect on instalments

An RD consists of multiple monthly instalments, and each remains invested for a different period. On premature closure:

* Each instalment is recalculated **separately**.
* Interest is calculated using the reduced effective rate.
* Interest is paid only for the actual duration each instalment remained invested.

For example, the 1st instalment remained invested for 16 months, the 2nd for 15 months, the 3rd for 14 months, and so on — the last instalment may have remained invested for only 1 month. All instalments are recalculated using the revised effective rate instead of the original booked rate.

### Why the final amount can be lower than expected

Two adjustments happen simultaneously:

1. **Lower interest rate** — the original RD rate no longer applies; the bank uses the rate for the actual tenure and may apply a premature closure penalty.
2. **Shorter investment duration** — each instalment earns interest only for the period it actually remained invested. Later instalments have less time to earn interest than earlier ones.

## How to withdraw an FD on Wint Wealth

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Standard flow">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the app">
        Open the Wint Wealth app.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Go to your FD">
        Go to **Portfolio** and select **FD**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select the bank">
        Click on the bank for which you wish to withdraw the FD.
      </Step>

      <Step title="View investments">
        Click **Invest Now**, then **View Investments**. You will be redirected to the FD dashboard.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Initiate withdrawal">
        Select the FD/RD you wish to withdraw, scroll to the bottom and tap **Withdrawal Details**, then select **Withdraw FD**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="FD not visible in dashboard">
    If the FD is not visible in the FD dashboard, use the direct FD portal link to view booked deposits:

    [FD portal — View Investments](https://www.wintwealth.com/fixed-deposit/redirection?action=webview&redirect=deeplink-manager/fd/VIEW_INVESTMENTS?section=BOOKED)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="View investments">
        Tap **View Investments**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Find the deposit">
        Navigate to the **Pending**, **Booked**, or **Matured** tab depending on the status of the FD/RD.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Initiate withdrawal">
        Select the FD/RD, scroll to the bottom and tap **Withdrawal Details**, then select **Withdraw FD**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Premature withdrawal may result in interest being recalculated based on the actual investment duration, and a premature withdrawal penalty may apply as per the issuer's policy.
</Note>

      </Step>

      <Step title="Complete">
        Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the withdrawal request.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
