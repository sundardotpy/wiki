## Dashboard performance

### Why is the dashboard displaying a wrong or negative YTM percentage?

This usually happens when repayments have not been updated correctly in the system. It can be fixed on request.

**Response to user:**

> Please allow us a moment to verify and correct the displayed value. You can verify your returns using this formula: Current Value minus Investment Value plus Returns Till Date plus TDS.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check for missing repayments">
    Check Finder for any missing or incorrectly recorded repayments for the user.

    **Finder:** Portfolio / Repayments section — look for entries that are missing or show incorrect amounts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Compare against the pricing sheet">
    Compare the user's cash flows with the **Daily Pricing Sheet** to identify discrepancies, cross-checking against the repayment records in Finder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Raise the correction">
    If a discrepancy is found, raise the correction request with the relevant team.

    Channel: `#cx-live` | Tag: `@cx-ir`, `@cx-TL`, `@cx-L2`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Verification formula (for agents)**

  `Gains = Current Value − Investment Value + Returns Till Date + TDS`

  Use this to cross-check the user's dashboard figures before escalating.
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Escalation** (if figures still do not match after verification) — Channel: `#cx-live` | Tag: `@cx-ir`, `@cx-TL`, `@cx-L2`
</Warning>

### Why is the current value less than the invested value?

There are two common reasons:

1. **Amortization** — if some of the principal has already been repaid as part of scheduled repayments, the remaining bond value will naturally be lower than the original invested amount.
2. **System update timing** — immediately following a repayment there can be a brief delay before the dashboard reflects the updated value.

In both cases this is expected behaviour and not an error. See [Basics of bonds](/product-sop-faq/bonds/basics-of-bonds) for more on amortised bonds.

### Why do gains change between days?

Daily changes in dashboard gains are typically caused by one of two factors:

1. **Amortization** — as principal repayments are processed, the outstanding investment value adjusts accordingly.
2. **Dashboard update timing** — the dashboard may reflect changes slightly before or after a repayment is fully processed.

## Dashboard navigation

### Where can I see all my investments in one place?

All investments are under the **Portfolio** section of the app or website.

<Note>
  Investments that are still being processed appear on the **Orders** page, not in the Portfolio section.
</Note>

**App / Website:** Bottom navigation → **Portfolio**

### Why is an investment showing as Pending?

An investment shows as **Pending** when the order has been placed but has not yet reached final settlement. This is normal — the investment moves to the Portfolio once settlement is complete. The current status and expected settlement timeline are visible in the **Orders** section.

### Can I sell my investment directly from the portfolio?

Yes. Navigate to the specific bond in the Portfolio section and tap **Sell** to initiate a sale.

<Note>
  The ability to sell depends on market liquidity and buyer availability at the time of placing the sell order.
</Note>
