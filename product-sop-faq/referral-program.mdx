title: "Current Referral program 1%"
---

You can earn up to ₹25,000 in total by referring friends to Wint Wealth. You can earn a maximum of ₹5,000 per referral. While you can refer as many people as you like, only the first five investors who actually make an investment on the platform will be added under your referral. Once they are added, you will start receiving rewards for them, up to the ₹5,000 limit per referral. The reward is calculated as a flat 1% of the referee's investment amount.

### Eligibility Criteria

- The person you refer must sign up using your unique referral link
- The referee must not already be a registered user of Wint Wealth
- The referee must sign up via the web platform (sign-ups through the Wint Wealth App are not eligible)
- You, the referrer, must have invested in at least one bond, debenture, or other debt instrument including pass-through certificates on Wint Wealth
- The referee must also complete a transaction in any of these instruments (transactions in other products do not qualify)

You may refer as many people as you like, but only the first five referees who successfully invest will be considered for rewards.

### Rewards

For every successful referral, the referrer is eligible to earn up to ₹5,000, with a maximum earning potential of ₹25,000 by referring up to five investors.

Wint Wealth offers a flat 1% referral reward on trades conducted on its platform. If the referee invests in any ongoing offer where Wint Wealth provides a yield benefit or discount.

Rewards are credited directly to the referrer's KYC-verified bank account after the successful settlement of the referee's trade.

### Reward Redemption & Fraud Prevention

If Wint Wealth identifies any fraudulent or deceptive activities undertaken to claim referral rewards, such as creating multiple accounts, using fake email IDs, coercing sign-ups, or misusing referral links, it reserves the right to withhold or revoke rewards at any time. Furthermore, Wint Wealth may pursue legal action and recover any amounts already paid under this scheme if the referrer is found guilty of such misconduct.

### Referral Sign Up Process

1. Share your referral link with your friend
2. Your friend must open the referral link directly on the Wint Wealth website (not the app)
3. Your friend can then proceed with signup on the website
4. This ensures the referral is successfully mapped to your account

Important: Do NOT ask your friend to download the app before using the link. The referral link must be opened before the app is downloaded.

You must have at least one successful bond investment to activate your referral link. Referrals work only via a link (no referral code).

A referrer can invite up to a maximum of 5 members.

### Earning Referral Rewards Criteria

- No minimum investment amount required to qualify for the reward
- No time limit to qualify for the maximum referral reward
- Referral rewards are only applicable on Bonds and SIP in bonds investments (not Fixed Deposits)
- No specified time limit for referees to earn rewards (they earn on each new investment until ₹5,000 max is reached)
- All referral rewards are credited within 5-7 working days of the order being settled in the referee's demat account

### Viewing Referral Details and Rewards

Once someone you have referred signs up using your referral link, their details will appear in the refer & earn section of your account.

- On the app: 4th button on the navigation bar at the bottom
- On the website: Refer & Earn section on the top right next to the Profile Icon

For each person added under your referrals, you can view their name, the status of their investments, and the total rewards you have earned from them. If you click on the individual's name, you will see a detailed breakdown of the rewards you have received from each of their investments, along with the corresponding investment dates and the reward status. This allows you to easily track both the overall rewards from a referral as well as the contribution of each investment to your total earnings.
