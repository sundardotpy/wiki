## Is the Family Account active for investing on behalf of others?

Yes. The Family Account allows portfolio viewing **as well as** investing on behalf of others.

Buy transactions can be placed by the **Primary Member** on behalf of the **Secondary Member**. The transaction can only be approved by the Secondary Member through their desired payment method. Secondary Members also have the option to cancel the transaction.

## What can I do using the Family Account feature?

The Family Account feature allows a user to view multiple family members' portfolios in one place. Transactions can also be initiated through this view — the Primary Member can place Buy transactions on behalf of the Secondary Member. The transaction can only be approved by the Secondary Member through their preferred payment method, and Secondary Members can cancel the transaction.

## How can I link a family member's account?

To link a family member's account, **both** the user and the family member must have completed **at least one bond investment** on the Wint Wealth platform. Once both accounts have an active investment, the user can proceed with linking from the Family Account section.

## Do referrals get affected by adding someone?

No. Adding a family member to a Family Account does not affect referrals. Referrals continue to work as per the original referral rules.

## Why can't I see my family members' FD investments?

**Response to user:**

> Fixed Deposit investments are not fully supported in the Family Account view at this time. This is especially the case for FDs created through the Upswing flow. Due to SIM binding, FDs are accessible only when the registered mobile number is present on the device. We have noted your query and will raise it with the relevant team.

<Warning>

</Warning>
