description: "CMR and holding statement requests, and how users can self-serve via NSDL Speed-e."
---

Two NSDL-issued documents are commonly requested by users: the **CMR** (Client Master Report) and the **Holding Statement**. Neither is generated by Wint Wealth directly — both are fulfilled by Ops or downloaded by the user from NSDL Speed-e.

## User wants a CMR

**Response to user:**

> Please allow me some time, I will check with the concerned team and get back.

**Agent action:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Raise with Ops">
    Raise the request in the `#cx-ops` channel and ask for the CMR.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share with the user">
    Send the CMR to the user's **registered** email ID.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Inform the user that it has been sent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Users can also download the CMR themselves from the **NSDL Speed-e app**.
</Note>

## When the CMR is needed internally

The CMR is also used by agents to resolve payment failures — for example comparing the IFSC on a user's bank statement against the IFSC on their CMR when a sell settlement has not been received. See [Liquidity](/product-sop-faq/liquidity) for that flow.

It is also part of the required document checklist for the transmission process — see [Nominee Claim](/process-sop-faq/nominee-claim).
