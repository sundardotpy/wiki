# **Product - SIP in Bonds**<br /><br />**What is SIP in bonds?   **

**Q:** What is a SIP in bonds?<br /><br />**A:** Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) in bonds allows you to invest a fixed amount every month in bonds carefully selected by Wint Wealth’s internal team. Each month, your investment will be allocated to different bonds, helping you build a diversified bond portfolio over time. The entire process is fully automated, similar to mutual fund SIPs, promoting consistent investing, better diversification, and offering an extra 0.5% discount on all SIP investments.

**Q:** Can I choose which bonds my SIP invests in?<br /> **A:** No. Bonds are chosen by Wint Wealth's internal team based on the SIP theme (e.g., High Return SIP focuses on maximizing returns). Users cannot modify or choose the bonds. This ensures diversification and operational simplicity.

**Q:** When will I know which bond I am investing in each month?<br /> **A:** You will be notified about the specific bonds 4–5 days before your monthly deduction date.

### **Why did Wint Wealth introduce SIP in Bonds?**

**Q:** What’s the benefit of SIP in bonds compared to direct investments?<br /> **A:** SIP encourages disciplined, hands-free investing and diversification across NBFC bonds. You also receive a higher 0.5% yield discount and can benefit from automated monthly investments without manual effort.

---

### **Who is eligible to use SIP in Bonds?**

**Q:** Who can start an SIP in bonds?<br /> **A:** Only existing investors who have already made at least one bond investment with Wint Wealth can access SIP. New users who haven’t invested yet are not eligible.

### **Investment and Payment Details**

**Q:** What is the minimum SIP amount?<br /> **A:** The minimum SIP amount is ₹10,000, and you can invest in multiples of ₹10,000 only.

**Q:** Can I change the SIP amount or deduction date later?<br /> **A:**  No<br /><br />**Q:** How will the actual invested amount be calculated?<br /> **A:** The actual deduction will be rounded to the nearest bond unit price, which may be slightly above or below your selected SIP amount.

**Q:** What payment methods are available for SIP setup?<br /> **A:** Initial payment can be made via UPI or net banking. For ongoing SIP deductions, AutoPay is required, either via eNACH (preferred for amounts over ₹10,000) or UPI AutoPay for 10K.

### **Payment & Mandate Issues**

**Q:** What happens if my bank mandate fails or funds are insufficient?<br /> **A:** The SIP deduction will fail without retry. You can make a manual payment for that month’s investment but will not receive the 0.5% discount. Banks may also levy penalties for failed auto-debits, for which Wint Wealth is not responsible.

---

### **Returns & Notifications**

**Q:** Are returns reinvested automatically?<br /> **A:** No. Returns are credited directly to your linked bank account and will not be reinvested automatically.

**Q:** How will I be notified about my SIP deductions?<br /> **A:** You will receive notifications 4–5 days before your deduction date with bond details, and again once the deduction occurs. Notifications will be sent via push, SMS, and/or email.
