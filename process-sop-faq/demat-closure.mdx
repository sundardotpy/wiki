## Account closure letter

**Response to user:**

> We will arrange your account closure letter and email it to you once it is ready. Please allow some time for processing.

### Checks and confirmations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Raise the request with Ops">
    Raise a request in `#cx-ops` and share the user's registered details.

    Channel: `#cx-ops` | Include: user name, mobile number, User ID
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send the letter to the user">
    Once received from Ops, email the closure letter to the user's registered email address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Users are identified by their registered name, mobile number, and User ID — confirm all three in Finder before raising the request.
</Note>
