# **2. HUF Demat Activation & Onboarding Process**


1. Once we receive the **signed sign-off from the HUF user**, the **HUF demat account is activated**. After activation:\n\n• A **dedicated Point of Contact (POC)** is assigned.\n• **Two emails** are sent to the user:\n\nOne email contains the **beneficiary details** to be added in the user’s net-banking app.\nThe second email explains the **features that are currently available**.\nAfter receiving these emails, the assigned **POC will personally connect with the user** to assist with queries, investments, and the onboarding process, ensuring a smooth experience.\n
2. **Services & Current Process**\nCertain services that are available to individual investors are **also available to HUF investors**, including:

* Buying and selling of bonds
* Viewing investments on the platform after settlement
* Receiving portfolio summaries and reports through the POC

  \
  
:::info
  Currently, the **HUF account is under development on the platform**, so transactions and servicing are handled **offline**. Our team will fully support the user during this phase, and the **HUF product will be made live on the platform soon**.

  :::



3. **Support & Communication**\n• The assigned **POC will conduct an onboarding call** to explain the complete process.\n• Post onboarding, a **dedicated WhatsApp group** will be created including:\nThe user\nThe assigned POC\nSenior team members\nThis ensures **clear communication, correct guidance, and smooth coordination**.\n
4. **Features Available Currently**\n\n1. HUF Exclusive Benefit\nAn **additional 0.25% YTM** will be applicable on investments made **within the first 10 days** from receiving the email.\n\n2. HUF Buying of Bonds\nBonds can be viewed on the platform.

   
   a. The user needs to share the **bond name and number of units** at least **one day before the trade**.\nUnit reservation is subject to the **Minimum Investment Threshold (MIT)** shown on the platform.
   b. The **exact investment amount (including decimals)** must be transferred to the shared beneficiary account.
   c. **UTR details** must be shared with the POC for trade mapping on NSE. 
   d. **Trade settlement slips** will be shared by **end of day (EOD)**.

       \
       
:::warning
       Trades can be placed **Monday to Friday only**.

       :::

       \
5. Dashboard Visibility\nInvestments will be visible on the platform **after trade settlement and dashboard update**.

* A **separate mobile number** is mandatory, which should **not be already registered or KYC-verified**, as the mobile number is a unique identifier.

4\. Selling of Bonds\nLiquidity transactions may take **4–5 working days**, as this is an offline process.

* All required **reports and documents** will be shared by the POC on request.
* **App-based downloads are not supported** at this stage.
