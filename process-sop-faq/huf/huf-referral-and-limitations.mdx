# HUF Referral

* **Referral feature** is not available currently due to the manual process. This will be enabled once the HUF product goes live.
* A HUF can be referred either by a family member or by a referee. An Amazon voucher will be issued to the referee once the referral amount reaches ₹5,000.
* This information should be shared **only if a user specifically asks about referring their HUF**. It should not be communicated proactively, as it may shift the conversation toward referral rewards instead of HUF investing.
* Please note that **a HUF itself cannot refer anyone at this point in time**.

# Limitations

* **Bonds that are more than 95% sold** cannot be reserved.
