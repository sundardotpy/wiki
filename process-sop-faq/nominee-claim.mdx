## Add a nominee when none is set

Share these steps with the user:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open More or Profile">
    Click **More** at the bottom left of the screen, or navigate to the profile section on desktop.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Account Details">
    Select **Account Details**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Bond Demat Details">
    Under that tab, click **Bond Demat Details**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the nominee">
    The option to add or update nominee details is available there.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Nominee update: single nominee

Use this when the user wants to replace their existing single nominee.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Internal check on Finder">
    Before doing anything, verify the following in Finder under the **SEBI KYC** section:

    * Number of nominees = 1
    * Nominee status = `APPROVED`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get email confirmation from the user">
    Ask the user to send a confirmation email from their registered email ID to [hello@wintwealth.com](mailto:hello@wintwealth.com) requesting the nominee update.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get the email screenshot">
    Once the user sends the email, retrieve the screenshot from the `#cx-email-coordination` channel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reset the nominee">
    Raise a nominee reset request — Channel: `#cx-api` | Workflow: `/nominee-reset`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask the user to re-add the nominee">
    Once the reset is confirmed, inform the user they can add their new nominee directly from the app under the Profile section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Nominee update: multiple nominees

Use this when the user wants to add more than one nominee or replace all existing nominees.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the number of nominees">
    Ask the user how many nominees they wish to add and confirm all their details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share the required forms">
    Send the following forms to the user over email:

    1. Nomination Cancellation Form
    2. Nomination Submission Form
    3. KYC Form
  </Step>

  <Step title="Inform the user about the process">
    Let the user know this is an offline process. Once the forms are filled and couriered to the office address, the operations team will process the nominee update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Related forms are available under [Nomination Update forms](/resources/nomination-update-forms).
</Note>

## Nominee modification

Use this when a user wants to modify any nominee detail, such as the nominee's address.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Inform the user it is offline">
    Explain that this is an offline process and they will need to fill out the required forms and courier them to us.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Direct them to email">
    Ask the user to write to [hello@wintwealth.com](mailto:hello@wintwealth.com); the email support team will assist with the required forms and steps.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Raise internally">
    Raise it in the `#cx-email-coordination` channel and request the team respond to the mail with the steps and nominee modification form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Transmission when no nominee is added

If the primary user passes away and no nominee was added, the bond holdings must be transferred through the **legal heir process**. Legal heirs must provide specific documentation to prove their claim before the holdings can be moved to a new demat account.

Guide the user to email [hello@wintwealth.com](mailto:hello@wintwealth.com); the support team will help them with the required steps, documents, and forms.

### Required document checklist

1. Transmission Form (Wint template)
2. Indemnity Bond (Wint template)
3. NOC signed by all legal heirs
4. Individual Affidavit for each legal heir
5. Original or notarized Death Certificate
6. CMR and Holding Statement of the deceased (available via NSDL Speed-e or Wint)
7. CMR of the legal heir's demat account (where bonds will be transferred)
8. Self-attested PAN and Aadhaar of the deceased (attested by the heir) and of all legal heirs

<Note>
  Templates are available under [Transmisson forms](/resources/transmisson-forms).
</Note>
