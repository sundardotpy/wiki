# NSDL to NSDL transfer FAQ


**1. What if I want to transfer NSDL to CDSL?**

To transfer NSDL to CDSL demat currently we are following offline DIS slip process only.

**2. What is NSDL to NSDL bond transfer?**

NSDL to NSDL transfer is the process of moving bonds from one NSDL demat account to another NSDL demat account.

This is only a transfer of bond units. It is not a sale transaction, so no sale amount will be credited to your bank account.

**3. Can I access NSDL speede app with same credentials?**

Yes, you can login to NSDL Speede app with same credentials..

**4. What if I forgot my NSDL Speede portal password?**

You can click forgot password on the login screen and submit a reset request. Once submitted, you will get a password reset number. Please reach out to hello@wintwealth.com from your registered email, and our team will authorize the reset request. Once confirmed, you can login with the new credentials you updated.

**5. Can Wint Wealth transfer the bonds from backend?**

No, the transfer has to be initiated by you through the NSDL SPEED-e portal.

Wint Wealth can help you with the process steps and can help verify your Wint Wealth demat details, but the final transfer instruction and authentication have to be completed by you on NSDL.

**6. What is SPEED-e?**

SPEED-e is NSDL’s online platform used to submit demat transfer instructions.

For this process, you need to use SPEED-e to add the target demat account and submit an off-market transfer instruction.

**7. What details should I keep ready before starting?**

Please keep the below details ready:

* Source DP ID
* Source Client ID
* Target DP ID
* Target Client ID
* PAN
* Registered mobile number
* Bond ISIN
* Quantity to be transferred

The source demat account should also be free from freeze, pledge, or lien.

**8. Where will I receive the OTP?**

OTP will be sent to the mobile number registered with your source demat account.

If the OTP is not received, you can regenerate it from the NSDL portal. As per the guide, OTP can be regenerated up to 3 times in one session.

**9. What is beneficiary account?**

Beneficiary account means the target demat account where the bonds will be transferred.

To give you an example, if you are transferring bonds to your Wint Wealth demat account, your Wint Wealth demat account will be the beneficiary account.

**10. Which option should I select for transfer?**

You need to select:

Off Market Transfer Instructions -> Submit Delivery to Client

Please do not use the normal Delivery Instructions option at the top, as that is used for exchange or market settlement deliveries.

**11. What should I select as Market Type?**

You should select Off Market.

Since this is a demat-to-demat transfer and not a market sale, it is treated as an off-market transfer.

**12. What should I enter in CC ID?**

CC ID can be left blank for this process.

It is generally required for clearing member related transfers, not for normal demat-to-demat client transfers.

**13. What should I enter as Settlement Number?**

You can enter any unique reference number.

For example, you may enter 1 or use the date in YYYYMMDD format.

**14. Can I select any execution date?**

No, the execution date should be today or a future working day.

It cannot be a Saturday, Sunday, or declared DP holiday.

**15. Where can I find the ISIN of my bond?**

You can find the ISIN in your broker app under the holdings section.

You can also check it from your demat statement or from NSE/BSE websites.

**16. Which reason code should I select?**

If you are transferring between your own demat accounts, you can select Transfer between own accounts, if available.

If the transfer is for any other purpose, please select the reason code that correctly matches the purpose of transfer.

**17. Can I transfer multiple bonds in one instruction?**

Yes, up to 7 ISINs can be entered in one instruction.

If you have more than 7 ISINs, you may need to submit another instruction separately.

**18. Can I cancel or modify the transfer after submission?**

No, once the instruction is submitted, it cannot be cancelled or modified.

That’s why I would request you to carefully verify the DP ID, Client ID, ISIN, quantity, and beneficiary name before final submission.

**19. How can I track the transfer?**

After successful submission, NSDL will show a SPEED-e Transaction Number.

Please note it immediately. You can track the status under:

Off Market Transfer Instructions -> View Delivery to Client

**20. How long does it take to process my submission?**

If you submit before 3 PM, it will be authorized on the same day. Submission requests made after 3 PM will be authorized on T+1 day.

**21. How long will it take for bonds to reflect in the target demat account?**

Usually, it may take around 1 to 3 working days for the bonds to reflect in the target demat account.
