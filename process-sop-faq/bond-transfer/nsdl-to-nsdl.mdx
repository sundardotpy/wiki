## Before You Begin: Prerequisites

* Use **Firefox, Google Chrome, or Microsoft Edge** with a stable internet connection. Try Incognito / Private mode if you face issues.
* Keep your **mobile number** handy, the one linked to your own demat account (OTP will arrive here).
* Have your **DP ID** (first 8 digits of demat, starts with IN) and **Client ID** (last 8 digits) ready for **both your own account and the beneficiary account**.
* Keep the **PAN number** ready for both your own account and the beneficiary.
* If you are already using the **NSDL SPEED-e mobile app**, contact Wint Wealth support before proceeding, your account may need to be reconfigured first.

## Part A: SPEED-e Account Registration

### Step 1: Go to the NSDL e-Services Landing Page

* Open your browser and visit: [https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/index.jsp](https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/index.jsp)

  \
  ![NSDL SPeede e-services landing page](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/3a08cc29-89aa-4acf-9240-da99239b2ad7/01-nsdl-eservices-home.jpg)


* On the left-hand side panel, click **New User Registration**.
* From the sub-menu, click **SPEED-e**.

 ![New user registration NSDL speede portal](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/93f15687-1229-4720-95e7-d5a6c30a1d63/02-select-speede-submenu.jpg)

### Step 2: Navigate to the Password User Registration Form

* On the SPEED-e Registration page, scroll to the **Clients** section.
* Under **Password Users**, click the **REGISTER** hyperlink in the line: *"To REGISTER for a password based access to SPEED-e:"*

 ![Screenshot](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/de756b7c-6c1b-4efb-856f-54e200fff561/03-speede-registration-clients.jpg)

### Step 3: Fill in the Registration Form

* **User ID:** Any ID of your choice. Recommended format: `WINT<ClientID>` (e.g., WINT12345678)
* **User Name:** Your name exactly as on your demat account.
* **Date of Birth:** Your date of birth.
* **Email:** Your registered email ID (preferred).
* **Client ID:** Last 8 digits of your demat number.
* **DP ID:** First 8 digits of your demat number (starts with IN).
* **Sole/First Account Holder Name:** Name as per demat.
* **CM BP ID:** Enter `IN548689` in the first CM BP ID field.
* **Services Available:** Tick both **SPEED-e** and **IDeAS** checkboxes.
* **Mode of Usage:** Tick **Mobile Application** and enter your mobile number.
* **Password:** Set a strong alphanumeric password (8–16 characters).
* Enter the **Captcha / Verification Code** and click **Submit**.

 ![User registration form NSDL speede portal](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/2329df98-b8d0-4af9-9cdb-27cdf394db63/04-registration-request-form.jpg)


### Step 4: Authenticate with OTP

* After submitting the form, you will receive an **OTP on your registered mobile number**.
* Enter the OTP and click **Submit**.


 ![OTP Authentication page](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/6d5fcdf4-8e21-4ae2-84a4-4078dfab20cd/05-otp-authentication-registration.jpg)

### Step 5: Confirm Registration Details

* A **Confirmation page** will display all your details. Review carefully.
* If everything is correct, click **Confirm**. To correct, click **Cancel** and go back.


 ![Registration confirmation for SPEED-e and IDeAS](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/4e3ae4ac-1bf9-462c-9e8e-104d33edf71d/06-registration-confirmation.jpg)

### Step 6: Note Your Registration Number and Contact Us

* The **Registration Successful** screen will show your Registration Number and User ID.
* **Note down your Registration Number.**
* **Contact Wint Wealth customer support** and share your Registration Number, we will authorise your account.

 ![Registration number post registration page](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/bdb39cb8-a46a-404e-ab17-de3312b69fa8/07-registration-successful.jpg)

## Part B: Login to SPEED-e Portal

* Once authorisation is confirmed, go to the SPEED-e login page: [https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/HomeLogin.jsp?loginpg=1&image=speede](https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/HomeLogin.jsp?loginpg=1&image=speede)

* You will be asked to enter an **OTP sent to both your mobile number and email ID** registered in your demat account.
* Enter the OTP and click **Submit**.

 ![NSDL speede portal login OTP authentication page](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/c1d954a0-0ad9-4283-a4d0-2b1ac544bc74/09-otp-authentication-login.jpg)

### Step 8: Familiarise Yourself with the SPEED-e Dashboard

* After successful login, you will land on the **SPEED-e dashboard**.
* The **left-hand sidebar** contains all the key navigation options you will need.

 ![NSDL Speede portal dashboard](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/62421042-b455-4d37-9e2b-e97386d17aaf/10-speede-dashboard-sidebar.jpg)

## Part C: Add Beneficiary Account

> **What is a Beneficiary?** You must add the recipient's demat account as a beneficiary before you can transfer bonds. This is a one-time setup per recipient. Currently, only NSDL-to-NSDL transfers are supported.

### Step 9: Go to 'Add Beneficiary NSDL'

* On the left-hand sidebar, scroll to the **Inquiries** section.
* Click on **Add Beneficiary NSDL**.

 ![Adding beneficiary NSDL speede portal](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/f521e18b-df3c-414b-a149-9526087cdd92/11-sidebar-add-beneficiary-nsdl.jpg)

### Step 10: Fill in Beneficiary Details and Submit

* Fill in the **Add Beneficiary NSDL** form with the recipient's details:
  * **Beneficiary DP ID:** First 8 digits of the recipient's demat number (e.g., IN304633)
  * **Beneficiary Client ID:** Last 8 digits of the recipient's demat number
  * **PAN:** PAN number of the beneficiary account holder
* Click **Validate**, the beneficiary's name will auto-populate. Verify the name carefully.
* Once confirmed, click **Submit**.

 ![Add beneficiary NSDL form](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/5d1cb9aa-02d5-455a-95c5-2777eb5f3fa4/12-add-beneficiary-nsdl-form.jpg)

### Step 11: Acknowledge and Verify Beneficiary

* An **Acknowledgement screen** will appear. Review details and click **OK** to finalise.

 ![Add beneficiary NSDL ACKNOWLEDGEMENT](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/6ec40d07-2484-42b1-971f-3f32ca0b6d2a/13-add-beneficiary-acknowledgement.jpg)

* To verify, go to **View Beneficiary NSDL** on the sidebar. The beneficiary will appear with status **Active**.


 ![View beneficiary NSDL on the sidebar.](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/ee8ecf50-1ef6-4944-bfc0-34364557f42f/Screenshot-2026-07-14-at-12.40.58-PM.png)

## Part D: Transfer Bonds

> **Before You Transfer** You can transfer up to **7 bond ISINs** in a single delivery instruction. The ISIN can be found on the Wint Wealth app or your holding statement.

### Step 12: Go to 'Off Market Transfer Instructions'

* On the left sidebar, find the **Off Market Transfer Instructions** section.
* Click on **Submit Delivery to Client**.

 ![Submit delivery to client](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/16274c45-05d7-4932-a05d-fb429dd7a3e7/15-sidebar-off-market-transfer.jpg)

### Step 13: Fill in the Delivery Instruction Form

* **Execution Date:** Select today's date or a future working day using the calendar icon.
* **DP ID & Client ID:** Select the beneficiary from the dropdown (auto-populated from saved beneficiaries).
* **CC ID / Market Type / Settlement No.:** Leave as-is, greyed out by default.
* **ISIN:** Enter the bond ISIN (e.g., INE0LN107071), find it on the Wint Wealth app or holding statement.
* **Quantity:** Number of bonds to transfer.
* **Reason Code:** Select **Transfer to Own Account** or the applicable reason from the dropdown.
* You may enter up to **7 ISINs** in a single instruction (one ISIN per row).

 ![Delivery Instruction form](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/77a4bf87-7887-4cf1-8ef5-3c73a7dde518/16-delivery-instruction-form.jpg)

 ![Reason codes NSDL to NSDL transfer](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/a308b0a5-2b37-47ff-9f07-0efe2fed7fc5/17-reason-code-dropdown.jpg)

### Step 14: Submit and Authenticate with OTP

* Once all details are filled, click **Submit** and **authenticate with the OTP** sent to your registered mobile number.
* Your transfer request is now successfully submitted. Wint Wealth will process and authorize it from our end.
* Once we authorize the transaction, you will receive a link to your email and mobile to authenticate the transaction.

## Forgot Password / Reset Password

> **When to Use This** If you have forgotten your SPEED-e password, please follow the steps below to submit a password reset request. Your new password will be active only after Wint Wealth authorises the request from our end.

### Step 1: Click "Forgot Password?" on the Login Page

* Go to the SPEED-e login page: [https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/HomeLogin.jsp?loginpg=1&image=speede](https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/HomeLogin.jsp?loginpg=1&image=speede)
* Below the login fields, click the **Forgot Password?** button.

 ![Forgot password nsdl speede portal](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/449cf7a3-7515-4aa2-95a2-78919126d544/18-forgot-password-login-page.jpg)

### Step 2: Select the Correct Option on the Forgot Password Page

* On the **Forgot Password?** page, you will see three options.
* Click the **"click here"** link in the second option that reads: *"If you are a Client accessing SPEED-e/IDeAS and have forgotten your password, Please click here to submit a password reset request to your DP."*
* Do **NOT** select the first option (Automated reset to NSDL), that will not work for our accounts. Always choose the second option to route the request to Wint Wealth (your DP).

 ![Forgot password nsdl speede portal option](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/30e9a6d0-d1cb-4b0c-8eed-0eda2a6ef087/19-forgot-password-options.jpg)

### Step 3: Fill in the Password Reset Request Form

* Fill in the **Password Reset Request** form with the following details:
  * **User ID:** Your existing SPEED-e User ID (e.g., WINT12345678)
  * **New Password:** Your desired new password (8–16 alphanumeric characters)
  * **Confirm New Password:** Re-enter the new password
  * **DP ID:** First 8 digits of your demat number (starts with IN)
  * **Client ID:** Last 8 digits of your demat number
  * **Email Address:** Your registered email ID
  * **Date of Birth:** Select your date of birth from the dropdowns
* Enter the **Verification Code (Captcha)** shown and click **Submit**.

  \

 ![NSDL speede portal password reset request form](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/f4cfe6ee-7608-4152-bd2b-fa6d733dd451/20-password-reset-request-form.jpg)

### Step 4: Note Your Password Reset Request Number and Contact Us

* After submitting, you will see a **Password Reset Request SUCCESSFUL** screen.
* Note down your **Password Reset Request Number** (e.g., 10192324) carefully.
* Now contact Wint Wealth in either of the following ways:
  * Send an email from your **registered email ID** to [hello@wintwealth.com](mailto:hello@wintwealth.com), mention your Password Reset Request Number in the email.
  * Or reach out to **Wint Wealth Customer Support** and share the request number.

    \

 ![NSDL speede portal password reset request number](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/dd16ef84-9b49-4d41-81d6-681dc16695cf/21-password-reset-successful.jpg)

### Step 5: Wint Wealth Authorises the Request, Login with New Password

* Our team will verify your request using your registered email confirmation and the Request Number you shared.
* Once verified, Wint Wealth DP Ops will **authorise the password reset** from our end via the NSDL back-end.
* You will receive a confirmation once it is done. After that, you can log in to the SPEED-e portal using your **new password**.
* Login URL: [https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/HomeLogin.jsp?loginpg=1&image=speede](https://eservices.nsdl.com/SecureWeb/HomeLogin.jsp?loginpg=1&image=speede)

> **Important Reminder** Remember: Your SPEED-e password reset is complete only after Wint Wealth authorises it. Please do not attempt to login with the new password until you receive confirmation from us. For any queries, write to us at [hello@wintwealth.com](mailto:hello@wintwealth.com) from your registered email ID.
