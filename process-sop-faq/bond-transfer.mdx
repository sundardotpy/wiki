NSDL Speede portal allows user to submit online units transfer request. Currently NSDL to NSDL transfer is supported & catered on online method.\n\nWhen ever you get a request from user ask them NSDL to NSDL transfer or NSDL to CDSL transfer, if they are not aware about this ask them to whom you are transferring which broker, you can search online under which depositary that broker is registered. \n\nGroww & Zerodha both are registered with CDSL as their depository.
