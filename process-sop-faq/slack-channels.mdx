| \#  | Channel | When to Use |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| 1   | cx-live | User is waiting and you do not know the answer to their query. |
| 2   | cx-api  | 1\. Referral Mapping<br />2. SIP related requests<br />3. Nominee Reset Requests<br />4. ACF (Account Closure Form Link requests)<br />5. OTP Reset |
| 3   | cx-clarifications | To understand product/platform/process for future conversations. **Do not raise queries** here when a user is waiting on chat. Can also be used for CX process suggestions and optimisation requests.Suggestions shared by user’s. |
| 4   | cx-internal-coordination | Do not raise cases here only because you are unsure of the answer (if unsure, raise on cx-live TLs will guide you). <br /><br />For processes/resolutions with no direct answers that need further investigation or coordination. Genuine cases needing external teams' help will be raised automatically upon TL approval. |
| 5   | cx-escalation | Any case needing escalation user is not happy or you cannot handle it. When TLs' or Apoorv's intervention is needed. <br /><br />Cases with no resolution within SLA (delayed) can also be raised here. |
| 6   | cx-live-updates | To update regarding your break and other activities, so people are informed of your availability. |
| 7   | cx-announcement | CX team policies or product-related announcements. Updates that require CX team action or behaviour change. |
| 8   | cx-discussions | Organisation-level policy or other changes/updates. Awareness only no action required. |
| 9   | Cx-call-coordination | Calling team coordination <br />Post call request if your’e hands are full |
