\n\n**Steps to Download Submitted Form 121:**


1. Copy the submitted Form 121 link.
2. Open a new browser tab and paste the copied link.
3. Click on the **Download** icon to download the submitted Form 121 PDF.

Please note that this section displays details only for those entities that are currently available on our platform for **online Form 121 submission**.

For your convenience, kindly refer to the attached [**Master Sheet**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1kKdtfdFijLyP7y_d6vCkK7VxKBx7a1X30KphQVovR_A/edit?gid=1162900448#gid=1162900448), which contains details of all entities and their respective Form 121 submission processes.

A **"Yes"** remark in the sheet indicates that the respective entity is currently available on our platform for Form 121 submission, while other entities may follow an email/portal-based submission process.
