## Step 1 · Try This First Hard Reload the Page

Forces the browser to re-fetch everything fresh from the server.

⌨️ Press all three keys together on your keyboard:

**⌘ Command \+ ⇧ Shift \+ R**

This clears the page's cached version and loads it fresh. The page will blink and reload.

Check you internet speed from [https://fast.com/](https://fast.com/) verify whether its your internet issue.

## Step 2 · If Step 1 Did Not Work Force Close the Browser and Reopen

Completely shuts down Chrome and starts it fresh.

1. Click on **Chrome** in the top menu bar (top-left of your screen).
2. Click **"Quit Google Chrome"** from the dropdown menu. You can also press **⌘Q** directly.
3. Wait for 5 seconds, then open Chrome again and revisit the site.

## Step 3 · If Step 2 Did Not Work Log Out and Log Back In

Refreshes your login session which may have expired or corrupted.

1. Go to the site that is not loading. If it appears blank or broken like the screenshot below, proceed to log out.
2. Look for your **Profile icon** or the **Log Out** option, usually found in the top-right corner of the site.
3. Click **Log Out**, wait a moment, then log back in with your credentials.

> If the page is completely blank and you cannot find the log out button, simply clear the site cookies in Step 4.

---

## Step 4 · If Step 3 Did Not Work Clear Site Cookies & Data from Chrome

Removes stored data that may be causing the site to break.

1. Copy the link below and paste it in the Chrome address bar, then press **Enter**.
   🔗 Paste this in Chrome's address bar →
   ```text
   chrome://settings/content/all?searchSubpage=app.robylon.ai
   ```
2. You will see **app.robylon.ai** listed. Click the **Delete (Trash) icon** on the right side of that row.
   **Reference Screenshot — Delete Site Data**
   ![Delete website data](/images/uploads/37e6ebfd-d371-4dcd-a64d-6385396ea651/8f9a7852-8e0b-47ee-aa2b-8afdab5844c3/Screenshot-2026-07-14-at-11.27.48-AM.png)
3. A confirmation popup will appear. Click the **"Delete"** button to confirm.
   **Reference Screenshot Confirm Delete Popup**
4. Now reopen the site, log in fresh, and check if it loads correctly.

> After clearing cookies, you will be logged out of the site.
