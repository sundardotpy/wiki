MSG91 is the SMS gateway Wint Wealth uses to deliver OTPs to users' mobile numbers. Agents use the MSG91 dashboard to investigate SMS delivery failures during login.

## When to use MSG91

Check MSG91 when a user reports they are **not receiving an OTP on their mobile number**, and you have already worked through the standard troubleshooting in [Login & OTP](/product-sop-faq/wint-account/login-and-otp):

* The user has confirmed they are **inside India** (mobile OTP is only delivered to Indian numbers)
* Network delay, DND registration, incorrect number, and carrier restrictions have all been ruled out
* Resetting the OTP in Finder did not resolve it

<Warning>
  Checking delivery reports is an **internal** step. Do not share dashboard details with the user.
</Warning>

## Checking delivery reports

Open the MSG91 dashboard and look up the delivery report for the user's mobile number to see details on the failed delivery — whether the message was submitted, accepted by the carrier, or rejected.

## Escalation

<Warning>
  If the delivery report does not resolve the issue, **raise to TL immediately**. Do not attempt further troubleshooting on your own.

  The TL sends an email to the MSG91 team with the user's details and the issue summary for further investigation.
</Warning>
